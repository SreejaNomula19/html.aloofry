Simple Aloo Fry Receipe

Potato fry is an Indian side dish made with potatoes, spices and herbs. Also known as aloo fry, this 25 minutes super quick dish is a fantastic way to serve spiced potatoes in your meal. In Indian cuisine, stir fry dishes are commonly referred as ‘fry’ so this dish of stir fried potatoes is known as potato fry, though no frying is actually involved in the recipe. Learn to make the perfect crispy & non-sticky spiced potato stir fry with the help of my easy recipe.

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 25 min
  • Preparation:15 min
  • Cooking: 10 Min

    Ingredients

  • 4-5 ALoo
  • Oil,Mustard Seeds,Zeera
  • Cuury Leaves
  • Onions
  • Tumeric,Chilli Powder,Salt,Danya Powder

Instructions

  1. Peel & Chop Potatoes – Wash, peel, and cut the potatoes into small cubes or slices.
  2. Heat Oil – Heat 2-3 tablespoons of oil in a pan on medium flame.
  3. Add Spices – Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  4. Fry Potatoes – Add chopped potatoes, turmeric, salt, and chili powder. Mix well.
  5. Cook Until Crispy – Sauté occasionally and cook on low flame until golden and crispy.
  6. Garnish & Serve – Sprinkle coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
